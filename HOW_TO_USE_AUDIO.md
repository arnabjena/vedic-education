## Tips for Best Experience

### Volume
- Use comfortable volume, not too loud
- On mobile: Use device volume buttons
- On computer: Adjust speaker or headphone volume

### Speed
- **Start with 1.0x** (normal speed)
- Try 0.75x if it's too fast
- Try 1.25x or 1.5x if you want faster
- Best for children: 0.75x or 1.0x

### Browser Choice
- **Best quality:**
  - iPhone/iPad: Use Safari
  - Android: Use Chrome
  - Computer: Use Chrome or Edge
- **Also works on:** Firefox, Opera, Samsung Internet

### Interruptions
- If someone needs to talk to you, click PAUSE
- Resume when ready by clicking PLAY again
- Click STOP if you're completely done

## Troubleshooting

### "I don't see the Listen section"
- Try refreshing the page (press F5 or reload button)
- Make sure you're on an article page (not the homepage)
- Check if you're using a modern browser

### "Nothing happens when I click Play"
- Check your device volume (is it muted?)
- Try a different browser (Chrome recommended)
- Make sure speakers/headphones are connected

### "The voice sounds robotic"
- This is normal - it's computer-generated speech
- Quality varies by device and browser
- Chrome usually has the best voices
- Safari on iPhone has very good quality

### "It stopped reading halfway"
- Click PLAY again to continue
- This happens rarely on very long articles
- Most articles work perfectly start to finish
