## How to Use These Quizzes

**For Children (Ages 6-12):**
- Start with "Easy" level questions
- Read the story/article first, then take the quiz
- Discuss answers with parents
- Learn from wrong answers (explanations provided)

**For Teens (Ages 13-17):**
- Try "Medium" level questions
- Challenge yourself with "Hard" questions
- Think about the deeper meanings
- Connect to your own life

**For Adults:**
- All difficulty levels available
- Use for family discussions
- Test what you've learned
- Refresh your knowledge
